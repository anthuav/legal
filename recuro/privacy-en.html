Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes what data the Odin app (the "App") collects, how it is used, and what rights you have as a user.

The data controller is Odin App. Contact details are at the bottom of this page.

1. Data We Collect

  • Email address — provided during registration or retrieved from your Google account
  • Recurring payment data — subscription names, amounts, dates, categories (in Cloud mode stored on Supabase servers; in Selfhost mode stored on your own server)
  • Analytics data — anonymous usage events (e.g. screen views, feature usage) collected via PostHog
  • Technical data — OS version, device type (collected automatically by PostHog)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Authentication and account management
  • Data synchronization across devices (Cloud mode only)
  • Sending notifications about upcoming payments
  • Improving App performance and stability (analytics)
  • Managing Premium subscriptions

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data on the following legal bases (Art. 6 GDPR):

  • Art. 6(1)(b) — performance of a contract — email address, account data, and recurring payment data processed to provide the service (registration, synchronization, notifications, Premium management)
  • Art. 6(1)(f) — legitimate interests — anonymous analytics data (PostHog) processed to improve App performance and stability

4. Third Parties

Supabase — account data storage and sync (EU servers). Supabase Privacy Policy

Google Sign-In — optional login via Google account. Google Privacy Policy

PostHog — anonymous usage analytics (EU server: eu.i.posthog.com). PostHog Privacy Policy

RevenueCat (USA) — subscription and payment management (Premium users). Data transfer to the USA is based on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCC). RevenueCat Privacy Policy

5. Selfhost Mode

In Selfhost mode, all payment data is stored exclusively on the server specified by the user. The App developer has no access to this data.

6. Data Retention

Account data is retained for as long as you maintain an active account. Upon account deletion, data is removed within 30 days. Analytics data is anonymous and not linked to specific users.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

  • Access — you may request a copy of your data
  • Rectification — you may request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure — you may request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — you may receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — you may object to processing of your data for analytics purposes
  • Withdrawal of consent — where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing

To exercise these rights, contact us: anthua.projects@gmail.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days of receipt (Art. 12 GDPR).

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. In Poland: President of the Personal Data Protection Office (UODO), ul. Stawki 2, 00-193 Warsaw, uodo.gov.pl.

8. Security

Account data is protected by Row Level Security (RLS) in Supabase — each user can only access their own data. Authentication sessions are stored in the device's encrypted Secure Store.

9. Children

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we determine that an account was created by a person under 13, we will delete it along with all associated data.

10. Policy Changes

We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries: anthua.projects@gmail.com
